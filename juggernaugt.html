Juggernaugt Enemy Details

The Juggernaugt is a large, heavily armored enemy designed to be a formidable threat in the game. It features a massive body with thick plating, making it resistant to most attacks. The Juggernaugt's primary attack is a powerful charge that can knock players back and deal significant damage. Players must use strategy and timing to defeat this enemy, as its size and speed make it a challenging opponent.
