CONTAINMENT POD

Containment Pod Details

This giant test tube is a spawn/starting point for the Juggernaugt enemy where it is contained until the player approaches. It is designed to be a menacing presence in the game, towering over the player and serving as a reminder of the threat that lies within. The pod is heavily reinforced and features glowing energy fields that pulse ominously, hinting at the power contained inside. The design is inspired by classic sci-fi aesthetics, with a retro-futuristic look that fits perfectly into the game's overall theme.
