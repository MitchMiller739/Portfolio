About Me

I’m Mitchell Miller, a 3D artist and Unity developer. I build low‑poly creatures and bots—modeling in Blender, grime‑texturing in Substance Painter, and wiring up their AI in C#.

Right now, most of my time goes toward my solo project Chronohell — a retro boomer‑shooter where I craft enemy AI, design encounters, and cook up neon‑soaked shaders to bring a retro‑futuristic world to life. On this site, you’ll find demos for my Creature Pod, Crawler, MiniGun Drone, and Juggernaut, plus a handful of quick tech experiments.
